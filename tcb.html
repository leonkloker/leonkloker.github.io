  • Skills: Tennis (obviously), physical and mental strength, coaching & mentorship

I started playing tennis at the age of 11 in my hometown Grötzingen in Germany. I basically fell immediately in love with the sport, its competitiveness, total accountability and also team components such as doubles. After playing competitively for most of my junior career, I started to coach and mentor younger players in 2018 at both clubs I played for, TC Grötzingen and TC Bernhausen, in Germany. Additionally to just technical tennis practice, the coaching team and I also incorporated a lot of conditioning and mental training in order to not just improve the tennis but also the general mindset of our young trainees. When I arrived at Stanford in fall 2022, I joined the club tennis team to get the chance to play at tournaments all over the west coast. I'm convinced I will continue to follow my passion for the sport for years to come.
